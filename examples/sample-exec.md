## Top Risks


1. **Stripe integration timeline underestimated** (High, Block) — Start Stripe integration in week 1, not week 3

4. **Beta user churn from poor onboarding** (Medium, Patch-fast) — Build guided onboarding wizard before beta launch

5. **Success metrics are unfalsifiable** (High, Monitor) — Redefine all success metrics with baselines and measurement plans


## Key Mitigations


- Start Stripe integration in week 1, not week 3. Allocate 2 full weeks. *(Founding engineer, by End of week 2)*

- Hire a part-time contractor for ops and QA by week 3 *(Founder, by Week 3)*

- Deploy Uptime Robot + PagerDuty before beta launch *(Founding engineer, by Week 10)*

- Build and user-test a 3-step onboarding wizard *(Founding engineer, by Week 9)*

- Redefine success metrics: 'paying' = monthly subscriber; NPS baseline from beta week 1 *(Founder / PM, by Week 1)*
